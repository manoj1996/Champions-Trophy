INDIA

The Indian contingent, riding high on their superb home season will be focussed on clinching the Champions Trophy 2017! A champion side led by one of the most passionate cricketers of the present generation are bound to give whatever it takes to retain the trophy!

Some of the key players to watch out for are India's new finds - Kedar Jadhav, Hardik Pandya and Yuzvendra Chahal.



AUSTRALIA

The Aussies can never be taken for granted in any competitive form of cricket. Led by a captain who is in the form of his life, they are sure to make CT 2017 an interesting one! The last time Australia clinched a title was way back in 2009; they are sure to up the ante come June!

Players to watch out for are Steven Smith, Matt Renshaw and Mitchell Starc.



SOUTH AFRICA

The Proteas are famed chokers, especially when it comes to big tournaments. With a great team of promising youngsters and experienced seniors, the South African fans will have a lot of hopes banked on their cricket team!

B De Villiers, Quinton De Kock and Kagiso Rabada will be primarily responsible to help South Africa lift the CT 2017 trophy!



NEW ZEALAND

The Black Caps, after having made it to their first final in competitive cricket in the Cricket World Cup 2015 come into the tournament with great zeal and confidence! With a refurbished team, the New Zealanders will provide ample competition to all the other participating nations!

Kane Williamson, Ish Sodhi and Corey Anderson are players that ought not to be taken for granted!



ENGLAND

England will be playing the CT 2017 in their backyard and are the favourites to win it! Being well adapted to the dreaded "English conditions", they are expected to come out on top, given their strong batting lineup and an intimidating pack of bowlers.

Joe Root, Moeen Ali and Tymal Mills are three players who can dismantle any opposition!



PAKISTAN

Pakistan is a team budding with young cricketing spirit and talent. The CT 2017 is a great opportunity to show the world that Pakistan is still one of the cricketing greats!

Mohammad Amir, Sarfaraz Khan and Wahab Riaz will be some of the players to watch out for!



SRI LANKA

Sri Lanka too, has a completely young cricket team with talented players who are yearning to etch their names into cricketing books. Winning the T20 series against Australia in the outback would have given immense confidence to these youngsters, who will be looking to make their country proud!

A few key players in their team include Angelo Mathews, Lahiru Thirimanne and Suranga Lakmal.



BANGLADESH

With the BPL gaining renowned success and cricket in the country being taken a lot more seriously, Bangladesh are being backed by their supporters to go into the tournament all guns blazing! Winning the ICC Champions Trophy this year will definitely be on their cards!

Mushfiqur Rahim, Soumya Sarkar and Mustafizur Rahman will be the backbone of the Bangladeshis!
